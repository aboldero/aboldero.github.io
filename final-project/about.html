Scarlett Newman

Scarlett Newman found her interest in fashion through her mother's deep-devotion to popular culture. She's now taken that interest and applied it to things like blackness, punk rock, identity, celebrity, and other things that mean a lot to her. She's also quite connected to being a true Libra and will most likely bring it up in conversation.

Hung Tran

Hung Tran is a Vietnamese-Australian writer. He is interested in fashion as both a personal and performative tool across photography, dance, film and other media. He graduated from the University of Melbourne, Australia, in 2014. His favourite designer is Nicolas Ghesquiere.

Coz Conover

Coz Conover is a California-bred queer black artist exploring contemporary femme-ism, futurism, black poeticism, rituals of adornment and the ways in which these narratives engage with brown bodies in our everyday world.
